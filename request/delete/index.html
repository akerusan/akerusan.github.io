MMDb Delete Account Request

You can delete your account by yourself or send a request.

1. Manually delete my account

You can delete your MMDb account at any time via the MMDb application.

First, log in and go to your profile screen. Click on the settings button, then at the bottom of the screen, click on the Delete Account button. Follow the instructions to delete your MMDb account.

2. Send a request to delete my account

If you cannot log in or for any other reason want MMDb to delete your account for you, send a request to girard.alex.1983@gmail.com.

For security reasons, we will only delete accounts linked to the email address you send the request with.
